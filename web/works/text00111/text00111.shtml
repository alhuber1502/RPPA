Infant Joy

 
‘I have no name,
 
I am but two days old!’
 
‘What shall I call thee?’
 
‘I happy am,
5
Joy is my name –
 
Sweet joy befall thee!’
 
‘Pretty joy,
 
Sweet joy but two days old –
 
Sweet joy I call thee!
10
Thou dost smile,
 
I sing the while –
 
Sweet joy befall thee!’
Composed:1784-1789
Published:1789
Source:Songs of Innocence (1789)
