Cradle Song
 
Sing it softly, softly, softly,
 
Sing a whispered lullaby;
 
Learn to sing the way the moon sings
 
Moving calmly through the sky.
5
Sing a song as sweet and gentle
 
As the fountain on the gravel,
 
As the bees around the linden,
 
Murmur, whisper, trickle, babble.
Translation:Ferber, Michael
