The Sky is up Above the Roof
 
The sky is up above the roof
 
  So blue, so soft.
 
A tree there, up above the roof,
 
Swayeth aloft.
5
A bell within that sky we see,
 
Chimes low and faint;
 
A bird upon that tree we see,
 
Maketh complaint.
 
Dear God, is not the life up there
