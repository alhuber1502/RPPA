Moonlit Night
 
It seemed as if the heaven
 
In silence kissed the earth,
 
That she would have to see him
 
In blossom-shimmer dream.
5
The breeze went through the meadows
 
The grain-ears gently waved,
 
The forest rustled softly,
 
So star-clear was the night.
 
And now my soul expanded,
10
Spreading wide its wings;
 
It flew through silent farmlands
 
As if it flew toward home.
Translation:Ferber, Michael
