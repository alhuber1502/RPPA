Half of Life
 
With yellow pears leans
 
And filled with wild roses
 
The land into the sea,
 
You lovely swans,
5
And drunk with kisses
 
You dunk your heads
 
Into the sacred-sober water.
 
Alas, where do I get, when
 
It is winter, flowers, and where
10
Sunshine
 
And shadows of earth?
 
The walls stand
 
Speechless and cold, in the wind
 
The weathervanes clatter.
Translation:Ferber, Michael
