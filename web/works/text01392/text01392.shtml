A Poem for the Goddess Her City & the Marriage of Her Son & Daughter
 
she builds her city
 
the white goddess
 
builds it
 
not on the sky or earth
5
but on a cloud branch
 
builds
 
three gates to enter it
 
one gate she builds
 
in gold
10
the second pearls
 
the third in scarlet
 
where the gate is dry gold
 
there the goddess’ son
 
is wedded
15
where the gate
 
is pearl
 
the goddess’ daughter
 
is the bride
 
& where the gate is scarlet
20
solitary
 
sits the goddess
 
solitary glances
 
everywhere
 
she sees
25
the lightning
 
playing with the thunder
 
the precious sister
 
with two brothers
 
& the bride
30
plays with
 
the bridegroom’s
 
brothers
 
there the goddess sees
 
the lightning
35
win it all
 
the precious sister
 
over her two brothers
 
& the bride
 
over her bridegroom’s brothers
40
& the goddess was enchanted by it
Translation:Rothenberg, Jerome; Pavlovic ́, Miodrag
