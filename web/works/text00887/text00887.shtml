The Balloonist
 
Upon the great blue ocean
 
That flows around the shining stars,
 
I’ve travelled in a rocking boat
 
And met the heavenly powers.
5
I’ve been lost in contemplation,
 
Escaping from all earthly things,
 
And drunk the ether of eternity;
 
I've known the writing of the stars
 
And in their cycles and their rings
10
Seen an image of the sacred cadence
 
That mightily draws every sound
 
Into the surging force of harmony.
 
But alas! I am pulled down,
 
I view just misty shrouds.
15
The bounds of earth I see again,
 
And am driven back by clouds.
 
Woe! Gravity’s stern law
 
Reasserts its case:
 
No one may withdraw
20
From the earthbound race.
Translation:Ferber, Michael
