Despair
[excerpts]

‘Ignorance is faith’, the world told me, and I fled from you with disdain when you returned, and either I call on you in my profound pain or you no longer come to afford a smile to a sad man. Hymns have ceased, the heavens closed down, [...] I exclaimed endlessly, ‘I want for faith’.

Translation:Cardwell, Richard C.
