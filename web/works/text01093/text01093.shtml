Withdrawal
 
Let me be, World, let me be!
 
Do not tempt me with love’s gifts!
 
Let this heart alone retain
 
Its own bliss and its own pain!
5
What I mourn for I don’t know:
 
Some unrecognized regret;
 
Teats forever dim my sight
 
Of the sun’s beloved light.
 
Often when I’m least aware
10
Joy will send its brightening flash
 
Through what made me so oppressed
 
Blissfully into my breast.
 
Let me be, World, let me be!
 
Do not tempt me with love’s gifts!
15
Let this heart alone retain
 
Its own bliss and its own pain!
Translation:Ferber, Michael
