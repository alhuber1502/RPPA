[249 "Wild Nights — Wild Nights!"]
 
Wild Nights — Wild Nights!
 
Were I with thee
 
Wild Nights should be
 
Our luxury!
5
Futile — the Winds —
 
To a Heart in port —
 
Done with the Compass —
 
Done with the Chart!
 
Rowing in Eden —
10
Ah, the Sea!
 
Might I but moor — Tonight —
 
In Thee!
