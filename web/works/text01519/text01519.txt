There are a thousand desires like this ...

There are a thousand desires like this, each needing a lifetime.
  Of my wishes, many were gratified, but far from enough.

We have heard of Adam driven from paradise.
My fall from grace was far worse, when I left where you live.

At this time they couple my name with drunkenness.
One more age has dawned that needs Jamshed’s cup.

Each time we expected sympathy for our failings
  the blade of disaster fell, and we were found near death.

Oh preacher, for God’s sake do not raise the Kaa’ba’s curtain.
It may hide one more idol in which there can be no belief.
