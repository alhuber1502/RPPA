Mine — by the Right of the White Election!
 
Mine — by the Royal Seal!
 
Mine — by the Sign in the Scarlet prison —
 
Bars — cannot conceal!
5
Mine — here — in Vision — and in Veto!
 
Mine — by the Grave’s Repeal —
 
Titled — Confirmed —
 
Delirious Charter!
 
Mine — long as Ages steal!
