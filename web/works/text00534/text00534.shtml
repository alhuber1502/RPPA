Words There are So Baffling
 
Words there are so baffling
 
They seem bare of meaning,
 
Yet cast a strange spell on
 
One’s whole heart and being.
5
There is in them passion,
 
Love’s hot, flaming madness,
 
The joy of reunion,
 
Of parting the sadness.
 
A word born of lightning
10
Drowned by the world’s noises,
 
Will never be answered
 
And stays oddly voiceless.
 
And yet fail it cannot
 
To capture my spirit:
15
At prayer or in battle,
 
I know I will hear it.
 
From church I will hasten,
 
Afire with its promise,
 
And out of the fray rush
20
To answer its summons.
Translation:Zheleznova, Irina
