Song (When I was loved ...)
 
When I was loved — in ecstasy, in gladness,
 
My whole life flowed like an enchanting dream.
 
Cast off by you — where is the ghost of pleasure?
 
Ah, your love was all the happiness I knew!
5
When I was loved — when, inspired by you,
 
I sang, by praising you in song I lived.
 
Cast off by you — my fleeting gift has perished:
 
Ah, your love was all the genius that I knew!
 
When I was loved — charitable alms
10
I carried to the dwellings of the poor.
 
Cast off by you — my heart is cold and empty!
 
Ah, your love was all the charity I knew!
Translation:Kates, Jim
