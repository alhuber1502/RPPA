O Cool Wood ...
 
O cool wood,
 
Where do you whisper,
 
Where my love has often gone?
 
O echo,
5
Where do you listen,
 
You who understand my song?
 
O echo,
 
If you sang for her
 
Those sweet dreams turned into song —
10
All of them,
 
O bring them to her!
 
Her whom I have lost so soon.
 
In my heart,
 
The wood still whispers
15
Where my love has often gone;
 
But echo
 
Now sleeps in sorrow:
 
Far away my songs have flown.
 
In the wood
20
O come my darling
 
Where I wander all alone!
 
If my songs
 
Have now all vanished,
 
I’ll sing for you another one.
Translation:Ferber, Michael
